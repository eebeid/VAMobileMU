The examiner uses this part of the examination to establish a rapport with Staff Sergeant Marcus by keeping his tone conversational. The examiner asks questions concerning Sergeant Marcus's service history and the history of his injury. With the examiner guiding the discussion with key questions, Sergeant Marcus is able to speak naturally about his injury and experience.

Notice how the examiner asks Staff Sergeant Marcus about the effect his condition has on occupational and daily activities. The examiner will use this kind of information when he discusses the functional impact of the Veteran's condition in his examination report.
