Appropriate Diagnostic Testing

Next

At the end of the physical examination, order clinical and diagnostic tests necessary to confirm a diagnosis or to assess the severity of a condition that were not done previously.

If not already done, X-rays of affected areas may be needed to show the location and extent of shrapnel or other metallic fragments association with projectile wounds.

If additional tests had been indicated, the examiner would follow up to ensure he reviewed any assessment and test results and explained them in the examination report.
