In this lesson, we detailed the examination process by following along with Staff Sergeant Marcus's C&P Muscle Injuries examination. The examiner started guiding Staff Sergeant Marcus through the examination as soon as he greeted him, and the examiner was prepared to correctly answer questions about the claim. You reviewed the procedures and considerations for key parts of the examination:

  • Greeting the Veteran or Servicemember
  • Conducting a medical history interview
  • Conducting a physical examination, and
  • Closing the examination.

Now that you've completed this lesson, you should be able to identify requirements for conducting a C&P Muscle Injuries examination.
