This lesson begins with preparing for a C&P Muscle Injuries examination and continues the case study with video highlights showing how the examiner guides Staff Sergeant Marcus through the C&P examination. The examiner will use the Examination Request and applicable documentation protocols, in this case, Disability Benefits Questionnaires (DBQs), to ensure he collects all required information.

This lesson also provides helpful tips for discussions with claimants. As the examiner conducts the examination, he is aware of topics he should and should not discuss with claimants.

When you complete this lesson, you should be able to identify requirements and process for conducting a C&P Muscle Injuries examination.
