Muscle Strength

The Medical Research Council (MRC) scale for muscle strength measures the patient's effort in relation to muscle strength on a graded scale of 0-5.

0/5 No muscle movement
1/5 Visible muscle movement, but no joint movement
2/5 No movement against gravity
3/5 No movement against resistance
4/5 Less than normal strength
5/5 Normal strength
