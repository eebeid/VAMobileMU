Other Medical Records

Before you start a C&P examination, you should thoroughly review all relevant medical records, including those stored electronically. This review is helpful for familiarizing yourself with previous diagnostic tests and treatments for a condition.

If any records utilized in your examination report are not in the C-file, you should include a copy of the record(s) with your report, which will be included in the C-file. Also, tell the claimant to submit a copy of the records directly to VBA.
