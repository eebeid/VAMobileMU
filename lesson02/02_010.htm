The Muscle Injuries Documentation Protocol

Next

Muscle Injuries documentation protocols are designed to prompt you to provide necessary information about, and functional assessment of, the claimed muscle condition on the following key issues:

  • Nature of the muscular condition
  • Location of the involved muscles
  • Functional impairment of the muscle

In some cases, the findings you make based upon the claimant’s history and physical examination may require you to follow additional documentation protocols. There are three situations for a C&P Muscle Injuries examination where this is likely to happen. If a condition results in limitation of motion of a joint, you will need to follow an appropriate musculoskeletal documentation protocol, or when there is numbness related to an area of injury, you will need to follow the appropriate neurological documentation protocol. You would use a scar documentation protocol to assess any significant, unstable, or painful scar.
