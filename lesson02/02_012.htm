In this lesson, we covered preparing for a C&P Muscle Injuries examination by thoroughly reviewing the Examination Request, available medical records, examination documentation protocols, and when provided, the Veteran or Servicemember's C-file. The C-file may be in paper or electronic form, or both. Current documentation protocols for review are found at an intranet address and on public websites. Remember, using a documentation protocol helps you gather clinical information needed for adjudication of a claim.

Now that you've completed this lesson, you should be able to identify preparation requirements for a C&P Muscle Injuries examination.

In the next lesson, Conduct the Examination, we will describe the steps for the C&P Muscle Injuries examination.
