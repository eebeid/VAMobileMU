All C&P Muscle Injuries examinations are generated by an Examination Request. Review this form prior to the examination, especially to see specific instructions or questions from the Regional Office (RO) or Appeals Management Center (AMC) that you must address in your examination report. The Examination Request provides you with information such as examination priority, which documentation protocols to use, and whether or not a medical opinion is requested of you.

The most common priorities for the C&P Muscle Injuries examinations are Original Service Connection (SC), Increase, and Review. You may recall from the DEMO General Certification Overview course that history requirements of your examination report differ according to the priority of the examination.
