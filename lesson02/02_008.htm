Pertinent Medical History

Injured while on active duty in Afghanistan, Veteran was involved in a mortar attack on March 11, 2012. Veteran has embedded shrapnel, muscle atrophy, and scarring in right upper arm. Veteran was first treated in Afghanistan, then evacuated to Germany for further treatment, and then was sent to the States. Veteran was hospitalized in the U.S. through October 2012 due to infection. Veteran has had weakness in right upper extremity since injury occurred. Veteran also has had intermittent pain for the last year. Veteran is currently in the process of signing up for care through VA.
