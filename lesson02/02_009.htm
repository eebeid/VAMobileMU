Always review the applicable documentation protocol before a C&P Muscle Injuries examination to ensure that you obtain and review all the information you need to complete an adequate examination report.

Without the benefit of documentation protocols, it is possible to complete a medically sufficient examination for treatment purposes, but an insufficient examination for adjudication purposes. When you follow a documentation protocol as instructed, you're likely to provide all of the legally required information for adjudication of the Veteran's or Servicemember's claim.

Since this is an original priority examination for a claim within a year of discharge, the Examination Request lists a general medical documentation protocol. Because Staff Sergeant Marcus has a muscle injury to assess, the examiner will also use a muscle injuries documentation protocol.
