This course is a joint presentation of the Veterans Health Administration (VHA) Office of Disability and Medical Assessment (DMA) and the Employee Education System (EES). This program will focus on the Compensation and Pension (C&P) Muscle Injuries examination.

Course Purpose

This web-based training course has been designed with the help of subject matter experts from the Board of Veterans’ Appeals (BVA), the Veterans Benefits Administration (VBA), and VHA to provide an overview of the C&P Muscle Injuries examination process. It will address a knowledge gap by providing updated information, including procedures to prepare for, conduct, and properly document a C&P Muscle Injuries examination sufficient for adjudicationpurposes. You will successfully address the knowledge gap by achieving a score of 80 percent or higher in the Final Assessment.

Target Audience

This training is designed for C&P examiners seeking information on how to properly conduct and document a C&P Muscle Injuries examination.
