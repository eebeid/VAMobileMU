Conduct the Examination

This lesson focused on processes and techniques for conducting a C&P Muscle Injuries examination. Videos based on the case study with Staff Sergeant Marcus demonstrated highlights of the case study examination. Considerations for the medical history interview and for assessing muscle strength were discussed and an examination walk-through detailed important steps. The lesson concluded with tips for closing the examination, ordering any necessary diagnostic tests, and addressing questions concerning the claim.

Document the Examination

In the last lesson, you were provided instruction for documenting a C&P Muscle Injuries examination. This lesson covered the basic requirements for a C&P examination report, including substantiating any diagnosis you make, and addressing discrepancies in diagnoses or between objective findings and a claimed condition. Guidelines for reporting a medical opinion were also provided. Finally, you were told that you need to be aware of your facility's requirements for the release of documents.
