Scope of the C&P Muscle Injuries Examination

Next

C&P Muscle Injuries examinations can assess a range of conditions. Some causes of muscle injuries follow:

  • Trauma
    • Projectile wound, such as a gunshot wound, shell fragment, etc.
    • Rupture
    • Herniation
    • Deep laceration
    • Other injury, such as a surgical intervention that causes muscle injury
  • An infectious/inflammatory condition
  • Neoplasm
  • An autoimmune condition, such as myositis
  • For obvious reasons, there is greater demand for C&P Muscle Injuries examinations during and following military conflicts.
