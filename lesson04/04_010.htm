Each facility has different policies governing disability examination reports and documents. These policies may include the method of delivery, certification, and contents, so make sure you know exactly which methods are required at your facility. Finally, don't forget to check for completeness and sign the examination report.

C-file Security

C-files are legal files and are the property of VBA. When you are provided with a paper-based or electronic C-file, you should keep in mind that the Veteran or Servicemember may only have access to this legal documentation when a regional office (RO) staff member is present.
