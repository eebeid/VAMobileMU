Remember that it’s important to identify the affected VA muscle group or groups where an injury or disability exists. When describing the severity of a muscle group injury, consider the type of injury, the complete history in the service treatments records, and objective findings noted in the post-service evidence.

Remember, if an injury, disease, or infection affects any system other than the muscular system, you must complete appropriate additional documentation protocols.
