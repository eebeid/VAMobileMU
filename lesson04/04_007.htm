Functional Impairment

Next

You should give as much information as possible on the effects of a condition on daily activities and any occupational activities, whether or not the claimant is currently employed or regardless of type of occupation. Consider only the impact of the claimed condition with an emphasis upon the limitation of activity imposed by the disabling condition (38 CFR 4.10).

Example:
Unable to sit in one place for more than an hour and a half due to back pain.

Report your professional medical impression, taking into consideration the Veteran's or Servicemember's statements. What the Veteran or Servicemember says may help you to assess the functional impact of the condition.

Functional Impact Remark

Here is an example from our case study of how to report the functional impact of a disability. Notice that the functional impact remark is in the form of a statement from Staff Sergeant Marcus.

"Servicemember reports that he is unable to do any heavy lifting such as lifting a full garbage can."
